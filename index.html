About Apollo Performing Arts

Our Mission

At Apollo Performing Arts & Academy, we're dedicated to teaching students to perform confidently, whether for personal enjoyment, family, friends, or public audiences.

Our program typically spans 3-5 years, depending on the student's age and experience. We offer both Regular Lessons and Advanced Lessons, with opportunities to join our Performing Teams when students are ready.

Located in Frisco, TX, our academy provides a comprehensive learning environment with supportive instructors who guide students through their musical journey.

Apollo Talent Shows

We believe performance is an essential part of musical education. All Apollo students are encouraged to participate in our biannual talent shows and award ceremonies.

These events provide valuable stage experience and help build confidence in our students. Performing Team members have additional opportunities to showcase their skills throughout the year.

Twice Yearly

We host talent shows twice each year with award ceremonies to celebrate student achievements.

All Students Welcome

Both regular and advanced students are encouraged to participate and showcase their progress.

Enrollment Process

Getting started at Apollo Performing Arts is simple:

  1. Trial Lesson: Schedule a 15-30 minute introductory session ($35)
  2. Assessment: We'll evaluate the student's level and goals
  3. Program Selection: Choose the right program for your needs
  4. Registration: Complete enrollment with $75 registration fee
  5. Payment: Submit first and last month's tuition 48 hours before first lesson

For questions about enrollment, contact us at Robinpandey@apollotunes.com or call 469-616-1950 (no calls after 9 PM, please).
