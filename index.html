About Apollo Performing Arts

Our Mission

At Apollo Performing Arts & Academy, we’re dedicated to teaching students to perform confidently—whether for personal joy, family, or public audiences.

Our structured 3–5 year program offers Regular and Advanced Lessons, with pathways into our Performing Teams for committed students.

Located in Frisco, TX, we provide a nurturing environment where musical excellence and self-expression thrive.
